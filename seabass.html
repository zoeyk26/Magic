Ginger Sea Bass over Wilted Greens

From Sandi Edwards!
Serves Cooking time Preheat oven to
4
30 minutes
400 fahrenheit

Ingredients

  • 6 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 4 5oz sea bass filets
  • 4 teaspoons minced ginger
  • 2 teaspoons garlic
  • 1/2 cup dry marsala
  • 8 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 lime quartered
  • 2 tablespoons thin sliced basil
  • Aluminum foil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 fahrenheit
  2. Cut 4 12" squares of aluminum foil
  3. Place 1 1/2 cup spinach on each foil and top with bass
  4. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon ginger, 1/2 teaspoon garlic, 2 teaspoon marsala, 2 teaspoon soy sauce and 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil over each filet
  5. Seal foil
  6. Bake 10 minutes, until spinach wilted
  7. Transfer to shallow bowl and cool 5 minutes
  8. Open foil and sprinkle lemon and basil
