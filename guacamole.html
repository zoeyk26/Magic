Guacamole

From Alana Darnell!

Ingredients

  • 2 California avocados
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 small chopped sweet onion OR 2 chopped scallions
  • 1 small jalapeno chopped-seeds removed (If Zoey is eating, no jalapenos)
  • 1/2 fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon fresh cilantro chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix all together
  2. Cover and refrigerate until ready to eat
  3. Put pit in center to help guacamole from turning dark. When ready to serve, mix up and dark will go away
