Turkey Chili

From Aunt Sharon!
Serves Cooking time Preheat oven to
20
30 minutes
Oven not needed

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces extra-lean ground turkey breast
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 (28-oz) cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 medium zucchini, diced
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans
  • 2 (15 oz) cans fat-free, reduced sodium belf broth
  • 4 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 green bell peppers, diced
  • 1 (1/4 oz) package diy chili seasoning

Instructions

  1. Spray a large nonstick skillet with nonstick spray and set over medium-high heat
  2. Add the turkey and onion and saute until browned, almost 10 minutes
  3. Transfer the browned turkey and onion mixture to a large soup pot and add the tomatoes, zucchini, squash, beans, broth, celery, bell peppers, and chili seasoning
  4. Bring to a simmer over medium heat. Continue to simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes
  5. Pour into a serving dish and enjoy!
