Parmesan Chicken with Balsamic Sauce

From Joan and Jaimie Lupow!
Serves Cooking time
4
20-30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 2 egg whites, lightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon skim milk
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • 4 4oz boneless chicken breasts, pounded to 1/4" thickness (or use thin-sliced breasts)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup non-fat lower sodium chicken broth (Swanson's etc. - comes in a can)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place flour on a plate, eggs and milk together in a bowl next to it, and a plate with parmesan cheese mixed with bread crumbs next to that
  2. Dredge chicken pieces first in flour, then egg/milk, then cheese mixture
  3. Heat oil in large non-stick skillet over medium heat
  4. Add chicken, cooking 3-4 minutes per side, until golden and cooked through
  5. Transfer to a plate, cover in foil, or place in a low oven to keep warm
  6. Add balsamic vinegar, sugar, salt, and chicken broth to skillet and bring to a boil
  7. Reduce heat and simmer 3 minutes, until reduced and thickened to a sauce
  8. Taste and add sugar or salt to taste
  9. Serve chicken with sauce spooned on top and sprinkle on parsley just before serving
