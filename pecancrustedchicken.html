Pecan Crusted CHicken

From Joy!
Serves Cooking time Preheat oven to
4
15-30 minutes
425 fahrenheit

Ingredients

  • 2 whole boneless skinless breasts, halved
  • 1 cup finely chopped pecans
  • 4 cup bread crumbs (or matzo meal)
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/3 cup mayo (lite)
  • 3 tablespoons honey

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 fahrenheit
  2. Rinse chicken, pat dry, set aside
  3. Combine pecans, bread crumbs, paprika, salt, and pepper in a shallow bowl and mix
  4. In another bowl, whisk mayo and honey together
  5. Coat chicken with mayo mixture, then roll in nut mixture until well coated
  6. Spray roasting pan with cooking spray
  7. Arrange chicken pieces on rack and back for 8-10 minutes. Turn with spatula and bake an additional 8-10 minutes (or until chicken is tender).
