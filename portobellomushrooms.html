Grilled Portobello Mushrooms

From Lauri K!
Serves
6 (large servings)

Ingredients

  • 6 large portobello mushrooms
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped rosemary
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 large cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 shallot, minced or 1 teaspoon chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup red wine
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon fresh black pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash mushrooms thoroughly and cut off bottoms
  2. Marinate mushrooms in mixture for at least 3 hours (overnight is best)
  3. Grill mushrooms and serve - Delicious!
    4. *You can use this marinade for any vegetable. It's also good for lamb
