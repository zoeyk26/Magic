Sangria

From Kerri!
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle red wine (Cabernet recommended, not necessary)
  • 1/4 bottle (1 1/2 cups) white zinfandel
  • 3 cups orange juice
  • 1/2 can pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 cups sprite
  • 1 tablespoon Grand Marnier
  • Fruit
    • Sliced oranges
    • Limes
    • Apples
    • Strawberries
    • Peaches
      • *your choice

Instructions

  1. Let ingredients sit warm in pitcher for 2 hours (not fruit)
  2. Refrigerate overnight
  3. Add fruit, ice and serve

Food/Appetizers That Go Well with Sangria:

  • Pepperjack cheese
  • Spicy flavored cheeses
  • Toasted bread with crumbled blue cheese
  • Chips/salsa
  • Nachos
  • Fried calamari
  • Fried shrimp
  • Spicy meatballs
  • Pasta dishes
  • Chocolate desserts
