Caesar Salad Dressing

From Rachel Rosenblatt!
Serves
idk

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon worehestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper (or 16 grinds of pepper)
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1-2 cloves of garlic (crushed) or 1/2 teaspoon pre-crushed garlic
  • 4-6 inches of anchovy paste
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese (Locatelli cheese is nice in this recipe)

Instructions

  1. Mix dressing with a whisk
  2. Dress salad and enjoy!!!
