Barley with Mushrooms

From Elena Ridloff!
Serves Cooking time Preheat oven to
idk
40 minutes
350 fahrenheit

Ingredients

  • 1 12oz package Goodmans or Manishewitz Barley shape
  • 1/3 cup onion soup mix
  • 1/2 stick margerine
  • 1 small can sliced mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Ball barley according to package, drain and pour back into pot
  2. Add margerine, onion soup mix, mushrooms and mix
  3. Pour into oiled baking dish
  4. Bake uncovered with 350 fahrenheit for 20 minutes
  5. Cover and bake for 20 more minutes
    6. *Total bake time 40 minutes
