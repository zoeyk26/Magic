Zucchini Pie on Quiche

From Jaren!
Serves Cooking time Preheat oven to
8
45-50 minutes
325 fahrenheit

Ingredients

  • 4 3oz medium zucchini
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 large oz garlic clove (crushed)
  • 1/4 teaspoon dill seed
  • Dash of pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 8oz can Pillsbury Crescent Rolls
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1 cup monterray jack cheese
  • 2 teaspoons chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Separate dough into triangles
  2. Fit into 8 or 9" pie plate
  3. Pinch edge together to form crust-peal well
  4. Wash zucchini - do not peal - cut into 1/8" slices
  5. Saute in butter on margerine (do not brown)
  6. Add seasonings
  7. Spoon into crust
  8. Pour on eggs, top with cheese and parsley
  9. Bake on 325 about 45 minutes until edges are brown
